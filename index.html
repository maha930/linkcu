ABOUT US

OUR HISTORY

Link is a leading non-profit student activity at Cairo University that has been empowering students since its establishment in 2017, Founded by Kamal Mohamed. Through our activities, students can learn, experience, and eventually decide which career is best suited for them. Our slogan,"Share knowledge, life better," reflects our belief that sharing knowledge and experience helps improve lives and builds a stronger community. Over the past eight years, Link has elevated more than five thousand students across different fields. Through engaging workshops, sessions, and different projects.

OUR VISION

To share knowledge for a better life for students during their university years and later on in the work field.

OUR MISSION

Narrowing the gap in the market between supply and demand of talents through providing comprehensive career-oriented services to contribute to the growth and progress of the Egyptian Market.
